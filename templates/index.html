Core Capabilities

The platform is designed for institutions that need clear review workflows, practical automation, and policy-aligned audit trails.

Context-Aware Monitoring

Detects gaze drift, multi-person presence, and prohibited object patterns during active sessions.

Tamper-Aware Evidence

Every flagged event is packaged with hash metadata and chain-of-custody records for audits.

Smart Clip Generation

Auto-builds pre-event and post-event clips so reviewers can evaluate incidents quickly.

Identity Binding

Session, candidate ID, and event payload stay linked from capture through final review.

Offline Recovery Buffer

Temporary local buffering ensures event continuity when network instability occurs.

Behavior Trend Reports

Summarizes anomaly rate by room, course, and session to support policy improvement.

Review Workflow

A practical sequence from capture to resolution, built for proctors and compliance teams.

STEP 01

Capture

Session starts with camera validation, ID check, and environment baseline.

STEP 02

Score

Signals are scored continuously and grouped into meaningful incidents.

STEP 03

Resolve

Reviewer approves, escalates, or dismisses with complete evidence context.
